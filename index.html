About Me

Technology has always been a part of who I am. I used to watch my father create games from a few lines of code on the ancient family Macintosh. And even seeing my first webpage load, and knowing that somehow, I actually created that. Tech, for me, has always been about breaking boundaries and creating something that encourages positive change, no matter how small.

I studied BA Socio-Informatics for that reason. My degree aimed to analyse how humans interact with technology, and it helped me see the incredible potential in people-focused technology through innovation. My work as a Python coach in an edtech startup that sponsored underprivileged schools showed me that when technology is used correctly, it can open up infinite possibilities for people who, in the past, would have had very few.

I found myself itching to delve even deeper into coding. Coding is a creative pursuit of mine -- one that challenges me, one that excites me and one that keeps me constantly learning. The _nology course has given me the outlet to create even more, to experiment even more, and has made me even more curious as to how we can use technology to create innovative solutions to the issues we as humans face.
